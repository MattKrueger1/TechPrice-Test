Your RFQ is split into separate lots — one per vendor. You can pick a different winner for each. Best for price-sensitive multi-vendor projects.
Who can bid?

By default, all verified resellers on the platform can see and bid on your RFQ. You can optionally require a minimum vendor certification level.

Authorized Most resellers

Higher tiers mean fewer eligible resellers but typically deeper vendor discounts. If unsure, start with Gold.
