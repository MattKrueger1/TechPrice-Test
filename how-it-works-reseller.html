🤝 For Resellers

How selling on IT Pricing Network works

Stop cold prospecting. Get notified of buyers who are ready to purchase right now — and compete on a level playing field for every deal.

The process
Five steps to winning new business
01

Apply and get verified

Submit your reseller application with your vendor authorizations and tier certifications. Our admin team verifies your credentials so buyers know they're working with legitimate, qualified partners. This verification is what gives your bids credibility and sets you apart.

✅ Verified status builds buyer confidence in your brand
02

Get notified of live RFQs

The moment a buyer posts an RFQ for a vendor you're authorized to sell, you're instantly notified. No more missing opportunities because you didn't have the right contact at the right company. Every qualified RFQ lands directly in your dashboard — no cold calls, no prospecting required.

🔔 Instant alerts for RFQs that match your vendor portfolio
03

Submit your best bid

The structured bid format makes quoting fast and consistent. Every authorized reseller sees the same RFQ at the same time — no favoritism, no inside tracks. You compete purely on merit: your pricing, your service level, your expertise. A true fair shot at every deal.

⚖️ A level playing field on every single bid
04

Communicate directly with the buyer

Have questions about the RFQ? Need to clarify delivery requirements or offer an alternative configuration? Communicate directly with the buyer inside the platform — all within the structured RFQ thread so nothing gets lost and your responsiveness is on record.

💬 Direct buyer communication in a structured format
05

Win new business and grow your reputation

When the buyer selects your bid, IT Pricing Network makes the introduction and you close the deal. Win consistently and your platform reputation grows — buyers can see your win rate, response time, and partner tier, all factors that help you stand out on future bids.

📈 Expand your customer base beyond your existing network
