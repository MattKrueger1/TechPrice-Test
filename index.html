The process
Four steps to a better deal

No cold calls. No chasing multiple resellers. One request reaches every qualified partner — creating real competition and true market pricing through full transparency.

01
📋

Post your RFQ

You already know what you need — just bring the vendor, SKUs, and quantities. Post in under 5 minutes and it instantly becomes your single point of communication with every reseller in the process.

02
🔔

Resellers are notified

IT Pricing Network instantly alerts only the highest qualified, authorized resellers for that vendor — verified partners with the credentials and tier status to deliver what you need.

03
⚖️

The market sets the price

Resellers compete for your business with their best pricing — no bias, no backroom deals. You see every bid ranked side by side and let true market competition determine what you pay.

04
🏆

You choose the winner

Review all bids in one place, compare by price, delivery, and partner tier, then select the winner. IT Pricing Network handles the introduction — you close the deal on your terms.
