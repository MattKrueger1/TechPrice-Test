How IT Pricing Network works

One marketplace. Two sides. A single mission — make IT procurement transparent, competitive, and fair for everyone.

🖥️ For Buyers 🤝 For Resellers
🖥️

The Buyer Experience

Stop chasing quotes from individual resellers. Post once and let the market come to you — with real, competitive pricing from every authorized partner at once.

01

Create your account

Sign up in minutes. Tell us about your organization and the vendors you typically purchase from. No approval process — buyers are ready to post immediately.

⚡ Up and running in under 5 minutes
02

Post your RFQ

You already know what you need — just bring the vendor, part numbers, quantities, delivery requirements, and any maintenance or support terms. Post it once. It becomes your single source of truth and central communication hub for every reseller in the process.

📋 One post reaches every authorized reseller instantly
03

Receive competitive bids

Authorized, verified resellers for that vendor are notified immediately. They compete for your business by submitting their best pricing — no favoritism, no backroom relationships influencing what you pay. The market sets the price.

📊 All bids ranked side-by-side for easy comparison
04

Communicate and negotiate in one place

No more juggling email threads with three different resellers. Every question, clarification, and negotiation happens inside IT Pricing Network — one central place to communicate with every reseller simultaneously, with a full audit trail for procurement compliance.

💬 All reseller communication in a single thread
05

Choose the winner and close

Review all bids side by side — compare by price, delivery time, partner tier, and terms. Select the winner on your criteria, not just whoever called you last. IT Pricing Network handles the introduction and you close the deal on your terms.

🏆 Full transparency, full control
💰

Market-driven pricing

True competition between resellers means you see what the market will actually bear — not what one reseller decides to charge you.

⚡

Unmatched efficiency

One RFQ replaces five phone calls, three email chains, and two weeks of back and forth. Procurement that used to take days now takes hours.

🔍

Complete transparency

Every bid, every communication, every decision is logged and auditable. Full compliance documentation built in — no extra work required.

The Reseller Experience

Stop cold prospecting and start competing for buyers who are already ready to buy. IT Pricing Network gives every authorized reseller an equal shot at business they'd never have found on their own.

01

Apply and get verified

Submit your reseller application with your vendor authorizations and tier certifications. Our admin team verifies your credentials so buyers know they're working with legitimate, qualified partners. This trust is what gives your bids credibility.

✅ Verified status builds buyer confidence in your brand
02

Get notified of live RFQs

The moment a buyer posts an RFQ for a vendor you're authorized to sell, you're instantly notified. No more missing opportunities because you didn't have the right contact. Every qualified RFQ comes directly to you — no cold calls, no prospecting required.

🔔 Instant alerts for RFQs that match your vendor portfolio
03

Submit your best bid

The structured bid format makes quoting fast and consistent. Submit your pricing knowing you're competing on a level playing field — every reseller sees the same RFQ at the same time. No favoritism, no inside tracks. Just your best price and your reputation.

⚖️ A fair shot to compete on every deal
04

Communicate directly with the buyer

Have questions about the RFQ? Need to clarify delivery requirements or offer an alternative configuration? Communicate directly with the buyer inside the platform — all within the structured RFQ thread so nothing gets lost.

💬 Direct buyer communication in a structured format
05

Win new business and grow

When the buyer selects your bid, IT Pricing Network makes the introduction and you close the deal. Win consistently and build your reputation on the platform — buyers can see your win rate, response time, and partner tier status, all of which help you stand out.

📈 Expand your customer base beyond your existing network
🎯

High-intent buyers

Every RFQ comes from a buyer who is actively looking to purchase. No wasted effort on leads that go cold — these are real opportunities with real budgets.

⚖️

A level playing field

Every authorized reseller sees the same RFQ at the same time. Win on merit — your pricing, your service, your expertise — not on who you know.

🌐

Reach beyond your network

Access buyers you'd never have found through cold outreach. IT Pricing Network connects you with qualified organizations across the country actively sourcing your vendors.
