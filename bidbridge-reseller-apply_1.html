IT Pricing Network Reseller Agreement — Key Terms

By joining IT Pricing Network as an authorized reseller, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Authorization accuracy. You confirm that all vendor authorizations and partner tier statuses you have declared are accurate and current at the time of application. Providing false or outdated authorization information may result in immediate removal from the platform.

2. Bid integrity. Any bid you submit on IT Pricing Network represents a firm commitment that you can deliver the specified products at the quoted price, within the specified timeline. Failure to honor an awarded bid without legitimate cause may result in a formal warning or account suspension.

3. Platform conduct. You agree not to contact buyers outside of the IT Pricing Network platform during an active bid process without prior consent. Circumventing the platform to avoid fees or to gain unfair advantage is grounds for permanent removal.

4. Ongoing verification. IT Pricing Network reserves the right to request updated authorization documentation at any time. You agree to provide updated certifications within 30 days of any request. Failure to do so may result in suspension of your account.

5. Subscription terms. Access to IT Pricing Network's reseller marketplace is subject to a monthly subscription fee. Details are provided upon approval.
